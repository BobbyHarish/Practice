Government College Of Engineering, Thanjavur is an Affiliated college under Anna University. Nestled near Thiruchirapalli-Thanjavur Highway Sengipatti. GCETJ Campus is located away from the odds of cities providing a peaceful environment for the Students.The spacious and the earth hugging buildings punctuated with landscaped courtyards and pathways are designed to emphasise the business ethics and or characteristics of an excellent centre for learning.The Campus hosts well planned academic blocks, computer centres, lecture halls, libraries, laboratories, conference halls, staff quarters, hostels and students' centres.
