Computer Science and Engineering


(About Us)

The Excellence, Relevance in teaching, emerging the new technology and ideas is the vision of the department. The Department was started in 2013 for the upcoming innovators.


The Department offers four year under graduate programme in Computer Science is intended to educate the student with latest technology and updation of advanced area of Computer Science.


The Department has excellent Infrastructure, Classrooms, Laboratory facilities for the goodness of the students. The necessary licensed software and internet facilities are available to all the students. The team of highly qualified and dedicated teaching faculties motivate & encourage the student to have depth knowledge for developing the software and hardware. Creating the Innovators, Scientist is the Mission of the department.



(Vission & Mission)



To be a prominent and renowned department recognized as competent Engineers in a myriad of application areas with research and innovation Ideas.

To articulate the new ideas in the field of Computer Science and Engineering with software and problem solving skills, leadership qualities, team spirit.

To impact the ethical responsibilities, helping the students to contribute positively to the economic well being of our nation and to tackle the complex technological challenges of the future world.
